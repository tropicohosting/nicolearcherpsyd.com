About

Nicole Archer, PsyD

I became a Psychologist because of my love of connection.  I honor the relationships I build with my clients. Seeing them create new insights, use their strengths, and thrive; despite their challenges, keeps me excited about my work!

I obtained my doctorate degree from the Florida Institute of Technology in 2012.  My internship and postdoctoral residency were completed at the University of Houston’s Counseling and Psychological Services.

The majority of my career has been providing individual, group, and couples counseling; psychological assessment, supervision, consultation, outreach, and crisis intervention within university counseling centers. I have also worked with client’s in women’s centers, community mental health, and inpatient psychiatric hospitals.

When I’m not working I enjoy trying new restaurants, going to yoga classes, catching up on my favorite TV shows, spending time with friends and family, and spoiling my two dogs!
